About

Tech-a-thon, the Computer Science society of Atma Ram Sanatan Dharma College, University of Delhi, brings to you its annual technical fest, Sanganak Vimarsh. The event is a platform for students to showcase their technical skills and showcase their creativity. The events are open to students of all educational institutions.

build@ARSD

A 48 hour online hackathon organized by Techathon at Sanganak Vimarsh 2022. For all the coding enthusiasts, this hacakthon puts forth a coding event aimed at putting technology to use for the progression and betterment of the society. The purpose of the event is to bring together coding enthusiasts and any and interested individuals to improve upon and build a project and come up with an idea on the theme “Tech for Good”, expanding on the positive impact of technology.
